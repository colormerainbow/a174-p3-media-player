The Benefits of Protein on Weight Management

The proper nutritional balance for YOU depends on YOUR personal situation and needs. Empower yourself with nutritional knowledge to maintain a balanced perspective and consult an RDN to understand what's best for you.

Placeholder

Key Messages:

  • Proteins are chains of amino acids, the building blocks of all cells.
  • Complete Protein food sources provide all 9 essential amino acids in the right balance.
  • Basal metabolic rate: amount of calories you burn at rest.
  • Thermic effect: amount of calories used to digest proteins is higher than for fats or carbs.
  • Thermic effect may amount up to 80-100 more calories burned each day.
    3500 calories = 1 pound of body weight.
  • A higher satiety index food is more filling and may prevent hunger longer.
  • Caution: Excessive protein can be detrimental.
  • RDA is 0.8g per kg (NOT per pound)!!
    You can achieve a higher protein % in your diet by simply eating less fat and carbs!
  • Athletes, body builders, and people in recovery may need more protein.
  • Don't overdo it! Calculate your protein needs to prevent excessive intake.
  • Clinical data for high protein diets leading to weight loss is still not conclusive!
